Pricing


Free to use! Pay what you choose pricing!

As an initial way to help fund this 100% open-source project, we currently allow you to choose how much you want to pay each month.

The more you choose to pay, the better we can make the service in the future.

We appreciate you using the platform and hope that you will make the decision to help support hook.io with a paid account!

Free Account Limitations

  • No Private Hooks
  • Limited to 50 Log entries per Hook
  • Limited to 100 Cloud Datastore documents
  • 15 second time-out on all service requests

Paid Accounts

There are currently no specific technical limitations on paid accounts.

In the near future we will most likely apply limitations based on usage with the option to upgrade.

Our goal is to maintain a fair and transparent pricing model which accurately charges users for their actual costs.
